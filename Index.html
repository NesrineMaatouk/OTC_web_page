About AlOmran

Al-Omran is one of the oldest and most successful brands in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia that is specialized in providing all types of ceramics and porcelain for various projects. It was established by Mr. Abdul Aziz Al-Omran more than forty years ago. It was transformed from an institution to a compa- ny, increased its market share and spread largely in the Saudi market. The main activities of the company include wholesale and retail trade of ceramics and porcelain. The company’s strategy for the future plan is to increase the number of its products and the varieties it others to include all building and construction materials.
